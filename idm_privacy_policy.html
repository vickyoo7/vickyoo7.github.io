Permissions used/Data collection in IDM

android.permission.INTERNET
This permission is required to download files and browse the internet

android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
This permission is required for write access to SD card

android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
This permission is required for read access to SD card

android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
This permission is required to get state of the network

android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE
This permission is required to get state of wifi

android.permission.CHANGE_WIFI_STATE
This permission is required to switch on and switch off wifi

android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE
This permission is required to pause/resume videos/ads on incoming or outgoing calls

android.permission.VIBRATE
This permission is required to vibrate the device when download starts, finishes or errors outline

android.permission.WAKE_LOCK
This permission is required to take system wake lock to keep the system active while download is in progress

android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
This permission is required to start the Smart download service (if Smart download is enabled)

android.permission.SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW
This permission is required to show overlay notification if(if overlay notification is enabled)

com.android.launcher.permission.INSTALL_SHORTCUT
This permission is required to create shortcut on home screen

com.android.launcher.permission.REQUEST_INSTALL_PACKAGES
This permission is required to install downloaded apks

Data Collection by us
We collect IDM app package name, app version, cpu arch and a unique device identifier to save important settings

Data Collection by our partners
Our advertising partners may collect ip address and advertising ID. Please read our partner's privacy policy @ Appodeal Privacy policy
